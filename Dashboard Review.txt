8. KEY BUSINESS INSIGHTS


1. The business generated total revenue of ₹12.99 Lakhs with a profit of ₹1.52 Lakhs.

2. Central region generated the highest sales contribution.

3. Furniture category performed best in both sales and profit.

4. October was the highest-performing sales month.

5. 2024 achieved the highest sales growth of 32.37%.

6. Clothing category has lower profitability compared to other categories.

7. High-value customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.


9. BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS


1. Increase marketing focus on high-performing regions.

2. Promote profitable categories like Furniture and Electronics.

3. Analyze pricing and discount strategies for Clothing products.

4. Create loyalty programs for high-value customers.

5. Use seasonal sales trends for inventory planning.

6. Increase average order value through bundles and offers.

and supports data-driven business decisions.
